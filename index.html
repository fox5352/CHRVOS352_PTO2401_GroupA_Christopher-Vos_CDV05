About Me

I am an aspiring front-end developer with a passion for creating dynamic and responsive websites. With two years of programming experience, I've developed proficiency in a range of technologies including JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, React.js, jQuery, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL. My expertise extends to building user-friendly web interfaces, backed by a solid foundation in UX design principles.

I approach development with enthusiasm, constantly staying up-to-date with the latest trends in the developer world. What began as a hobby has evolved into a deep-seated passion, driving me to continuously expand my skills and knowledge. I'm eager to contribute my abilities to innovative web solutions and grow as a professional in the field of web development.
